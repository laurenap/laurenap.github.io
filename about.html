About Me

Hi, I'm Lauren Pearson, an undergraduate student at the University of Toronto with a passion for democratizing neuroscience scientific research and artificial intelligence. My journey began with a curiosity for how the brain works, which led me to pursue studies in neuroscience and computational biology.

I have worked on various projects, including developing a reaction-diffusion model for simulating tumor growth and exploring high-content image-based assays like Cell Painting. My goal is to contribute to the field of drug discovery and improve our understanding of complex biological systems.
